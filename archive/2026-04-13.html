Supply chain impacts on semiconductor manufacturing:

  • Helium: Qatar Ras Laffan (1/3 world supply) offline 3–5 years. Critical for semiconductor lithography and heat management.

  • Copper: ~40,000 tons/month cathode trapped in Gulf. Iran's 114K tons/yr refined copper halted.
