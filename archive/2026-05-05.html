Why it matters:

  • Silicon substitution intensifies — third merchant non-NVDA path. AMD's "tens of $B 2027 DC AI" implicit guide of $30B+ stacks on top of NVDA $200B+ trajectory and AVGO $100B 2027 — total accelerator silicon market trending toward $300B+ in 2027.

  • TrendForce 5/4: SK Hynix LTA structure with MSFT and GOOG includes:

Buyers are paying premiums (advance cash + accepting price floors) to secure supply — revealed preference for tight supply through 2027–2030.
  • MU getting paid same way: Hyperscalers paying Micron for HBM3e + DDR5 capacity carve-outs at similar premium structure. The cycle isn't peaking on contract pricing, it's extending.
  • P015 (DRAM undersupply through C3Q26): With LTAs locking pricing through 2027–2030 and min-price clauses, the "Q/Q price decline before C4Q26" failure path requires contract breach or material recession. Upgrade 88% → 92%.
