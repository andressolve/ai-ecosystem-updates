2. SK Hynix Q1 earnings call — HBM4E roadmap pulled forward, shortage "to worsen in 2H26"

  • HBM4E engineering samples 2H26, mass production 2027 — ~2 quarters earlier than prior roadmap. Driven by NVIDIA Rubin / AMD MI450 customer pull, not tech readiness.
  • M15X fab initial ~10,000 wpm — small vs M16 scale discussed for 2027+ (~100k+ wpm). Underscores supply response is slow.
  • Management guidance: "HBM4 supply shortage to worsen in 2H26 vs 1H26." Rare — usually vendors guide toward normalization to avoid customer over-ordering.
  • ~70% HBM allocation to NVIDIA Rubin. Remaining split AMD / hyperscaler XPU.
  • Cheongju packaging plant (4/22 groundbreaking, 19T won) = 2027+ volume. Samsung +50% HBM capacity targeting end-2026 / 2027.
  • NAND ASP +70–81% QoQ per pre-print whispers; Q2 guide bullish on both DRAM and NAND.

Implications: HBM pricing power sub-thesis reinforced. P015 (DRAM undersupply through C3Q26) held at 88%. P021 (MU FQ3 ≥$33B) held at 88%. Memory-as-binding-constraint picture deepens — supply response real but slow.

3. TSMC confirms no High-NA EUV insertion through 2029 — ASML -3%

Q1 shipments 2 High-NA vs 60 Low-NA ASML -3% Mkt cap hit ~€14.3B
  • TSMC will ramp N2 → A16 → A14 on multi-patterning Low-NA, not High-NA.
  • Commercial High-NA volumes deferred to 2029+ (A10 node earliest).
  • Q1 2026 actual: 2 High-NA vs 60 Low-NA shipped. 2026 full-year ~1/30 High-NA mix.

Disconfirming for the "ASML has durable pricing power because High-NA is physically required" sub-claim. The marginal tool of the next node is cheaper Low-NA — not the highest-ASP machine ASML makes. Unit economics for ASML top-tier tools compress vs prior bull case. Does NOT change aggregate wafer demand — TSMC is still adding N2/A16 capacity aggressively. Reshapes which layer of the stack captures the most rent from the AI buildout.

Logged as Moderate disconfirming evidence in theses/computational-abundance.md (2026-04-24 entry).

4. OpenAI GPT-5.5 released — API pricing ~2x vs GPT-5

  • First flagship price RAISE from OpenAI (vs prior pattern of flat or cut per generation)
  • Full rollout to ChatGPT Plus / Pro in progress
