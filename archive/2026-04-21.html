What Happened

1. Amazon × Anthropic: $25B + $100B + 5 GW Trainium The Story

Announced Monday 2026-04-20 after close.

  • Amazon investing $5B immediately, plus up to $20B milestone-gated — on top of prior $8B (total potential $33B)
  • Anthropic commits $100B+ on AWS over 10 years
  • Anthropic secures up to 5 GW of capacity for Claude training/inference
  • Near 1 GW Trainium2 + Trainium3 online by end-2026; covers T2/T3/T4 + future
  • "Meaningful expansion of international inference" in Asia/Europe
Why it matters
  • Two months after the Amazon–OpenAI $50B deal — second "mirror" commitment
  • Anthropic compute stack: 1 GW TPU 2026 / 3+ GW 2027 + 3.5 GW Broadcom XPU 2027 + now 5 GW AWS Trainium → most diversified frontier lab, only incidental NVIDIA exposure
  • One lab alone now >10 GW committed non-NVIDIA — largest single infra buildout in history gets larger
  • Marvell/Astera Labs surged on the news (interconnect/PCIe switch read-through)
  • P007 note: P007 is about a second lab beyond Anthropic, so this deal is not a direct P007 input. The 80% upgrade rests on OpenAI's 10 GW Broadcom + 2 GW Trainium = ~12 GW non-NVIDIA already committed.
Bear case / what am I missing?
  • Diversification is also a hedge against NVIDIA allocation. If NVIDIA still has best $/token, this is expensive insurance.
  • 5 GW "secured" ≠ 5 GW deployed. $100B over 10 years; execution risk real.
  • $25B investment + $100B flowing right back through AWS capacity is accounting-elegant but capital-intensive for Amazon.
